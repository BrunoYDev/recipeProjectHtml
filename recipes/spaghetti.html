Spaghetti

Recipe:

Ingredients (6 servings):

  • 1 package of 500 g Barilla spaghetti nº 3
  • 1 tray of cherry tomatoes
  • 1 whole head of garlic
  • Quality olive oil (enough to cover the bottom of the pan)
  • Chopped olives (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil
  • A dash of oil
  • 50 g of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Wash the cherry tomatoes, basil, and olives (if using).
  2. Slice the olives into small rounds and halve the cherry tomatoes; set aside.
  3. Boil 2 liters of water in a large pot.
  4. Add a tablespoon of salt and a dash of oil to the boiling water to prevent the pasta from sticking.
  5. While the water is boiling, microwave the whole head of garlic for 30 seconds (this helps peel the cloves more easily).
  6. Tip: Be careful when removing the garlic from the microwave, it will be hot. Wait a few seconds and make a small cut on the skin of each garlic clove; the skin will come off easily, saving time.
  7. Chop the garlic and set aside.
  8. Cook the spaghetti in the boiling water for approximately 5 minutes to be al dente (cook for 7 minutes if you prefer it more done).
  9. While the spaghetti cooks, heat a large frying pan.
  10. Add the olive oil and garlic to the pan for about 2 minutes (do not let the garlic brown as it will make the dish bitter).
  11. Add the cherry tomatoes and olives, and sauté for 2 minutes.
  12. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  13. Drain the pasta and add it to the frying pan.
  14. Add the basil leaves and grated cheese.
  15. Stir for 2 minutes to absorb the flavors and serve hot.

Ready to serve!

Orange With Eggs

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 large navel oranges
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil (optional, for dressing)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the oranges:
    • Peel the oranges and remove the white pith.
    • Cut the oranges into thin slices or segments. Set aside.
  2. Boil the eggs:
    • Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
    • Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes.
    • Remove the eggs from the water and let them cool. Peel and slice them.
  3. Assemble the salad:
    • Arrange the orange slices and egg slices on a serving platter.
    • Scatter the thinly sliced red onion over the top.
    • Sprinkle with chopped parsley.
  4. Season and dress:
    • Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Drizzle with a little olive oil if desired.

Ready to serve!
